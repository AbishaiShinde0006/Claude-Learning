Delegation helps us work effectively and efficiently by distributing work between humans and AI to make best use of both.
3 steps:

Problem awareness: be clear about what problem you are solving and what your goal is
Platform awareness: understand which AI model best suits your task
Task delegation: think critically about where humans are better and where AI is better. Don't give everything to AI blindly

Important: never share unnecessary or confidential information with AI.
