Product discernment: Checking whether the AI has given you the right information or its speaking about something unrelated

Process discernment: Evaluating the quality of AI outputs, Whether it is giving you what you want and how you want it

Performance discernment: Evaluating how well the AI interacts with you during the process

Bonus tip: Only discernment alone is not enough, you also have to give feedback to AI and do keep revaluating the outputs until you get what you want

Excercise: Activity Goal
Practice Product, Process, and Performance Discernment by evaluating AI-generated content in a domain where you have expertise, recognizing how your knowledge enhances your ability to critically assess AI outputs.
